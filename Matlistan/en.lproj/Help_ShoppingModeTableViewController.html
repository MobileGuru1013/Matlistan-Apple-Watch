Use this screen when you visit a grocery store.
Tap on an item to mark it as picked.
When an item has been picked, a blue Update button appears below. Clicking that button moves all picked items to the bottom of your list.
When you are done shopping, click on Back button.
All picked items will automatically disappear after a couple of hours. Items not picked will remain in your list, so you don't forget to buy them another time.
The app features an advanced algorithm that learns store layouts and uses that information to automatically sort your grocery list. To help the app learn the layout of your grocery store, please consider to:
  • Let the app know which grocery store you are visiting, by making sure that the store is selected in the top menu.
  • Tap items in the same order as you pick them, near their physical location.
