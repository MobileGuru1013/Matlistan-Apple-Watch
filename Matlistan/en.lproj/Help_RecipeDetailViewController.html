This screen shows the details of a recipe. Change text size by pinching with two fingers. Rotate the screen to show recipe instructions under or below ingredients.
Tap on the calendar menu icon to plan the recipe and add ingredients to your grocery list.
All underlined green texts are clickable links:
  • The source link opens the recipe web page.
  • The yield link lets you select another recipe yield and recalculate ingredient amounts.
  • A link in tags searches for other recipes that have the same tag.
  • A link in ingredients list adds the item to your grocery list.
  • A link in instructions starts a timer.
